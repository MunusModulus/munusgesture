Rocker Gestures

Configure rocker gestures such as right-click while holding left-click.
Does not affect existing gestures or dragging.

Rocker Gesture Guard Time (ms)

ms
The "guard time" to prevent the context menu from appearing accidentally after a rocker gesture.
Warning: If this number is too long, normal click and right-click operations will not be accepted for a certain period of time immediately after the action, making the browser feel frozen.
